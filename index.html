About Us

Gaia Energy, Indonesia's leading renewable energy company, committed to driving sustainable transformation. We focus on developing innovative solutions in geothermal and bioenergy, empowering communities while reducing carbon emissions.

Gaia Care

Gaia Care is the corporate social responsibility (CSR) arm of Gaia Energy, dedicated to creating a positive and lasting impact on communities and the environment. Through Gaia Care, we focus on initiatives that promote education, environmental conservation, community empowerment, and sustainable development.

By working closely with local stakeholders, we strive to build inclusive programs that enhance well-being, preserve natural resources, and foster long-term resilience. Gaia Care reflects our commitment to not only driving renewable energy growth but also nurturing the people and ecosystems that make our mission possible.
