Who Are We?

Who Are We

Gaia Energy, Indonesia's leading renewable energy company, committed to driving sustainable transformation. We focus on developing innovative solutions in geothermal and bioenergy, empowering communities while reducing carbon emissions.

Our Values

At Gaia Energy, we are driven by integrity, a deep commitment to sustainability, and the spirit of innovation. We believe that real impact comes through collaboration, working together to create renewable energy solutions that empower people and protect the planet.

Our Vision

To become a leading force in advancing Indonesia’s renewable energy sector, transforming geothermal and bioenergy into sustainable solutions that empower communities, protect the environment, and inspire global change.

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness Indonesia’s natural potential through geothermal and bioenergy solutions, providing clean and reliable energy for generations to come.
